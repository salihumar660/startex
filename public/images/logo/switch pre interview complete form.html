Popular Courses

Can’t decide which course to choose? Take a look at the most popular courses in 2023 learn. Browse Popular Courses. 

Primavera P6

Oracle Primavera P6 is one of the most powerful project management software products. It is designed to plan and manage various complex projects while meeting every team member’s planning requirements.

Primavera Risk Analysis

Each endeavour contains potential weaknesses or risks. Oracle’s Primavera Risk Analysis provides the tools for risk avoidance planning. It enables businesses to map and model risks and analyse the cost of mitigating them, significantly reducing the uncertainty associated with project and portfolio management.

MS Project Professional

Microsoft Project is the most popular project management software in the world due to its user-friendliness and ability to administer a variety of applications. MS Project must be utilised to effectively plan and execute projects.

Primavera EPPM

With Oracle Primavera Enterprise Project Portfolio Management (EPPM) Solutions, project-driven businesses can effectively manage projects and programmes. EPPM Solutions’ project management, communication, and control capabilities are essential for managing change and concluding projects on time, within budget, and with the desired quality and design.

SAP Business One

The SAP Business One ERP software platform was designed predominantly for small and medium-sized businesses. SAP Business One, also known as SAP B1, is based on the premise that smaller businesses do not require the same type of ERP as large, complex enterprises in order to operate their operations.

Quickbooks Enterprise

QuickBooks Enterprise provides comprehensive accounting solutions without requiring the installation of an ERP system. QuickBooks Enterprise provides expanding businesses and non-profits with a specialised accounting platform that combines payroll, parables, inventory monitoring, and more.
